Crimson rules

One primary CTA per section. Active links, hover states, pull quote accents, form focus rings. Never use as body text colour. Always pair with white or parchment — never with harvest gold in the same component (they compete).

Harvest Gold rules

Callout band backgrounds, stat highlights, announcement bars, badge fills. Never as text colour — always use ink (#141413) on gold backgrounds. Contrast ratio: 12.4:1. Used in dark sections as the warm accent against ink.

Updated design principles

01 / CRIMSON

One raised voice per section.

Crimson is action. One CTA, one stat highlight, one hover state. The moment it appears in more than one place per viewport, it loses authority.

02 / GOLD

Warmth in background, never foreground.

Harvest gold works as a band, a callout surface, a badge fill. It never works as body text. Put ink on it. Let it carry the weight from below.

03 / SAGE

The mediating third voice.

Sage separates crimson and gold. It's the environmental and agricultural register — used when the brand speaks about the land, not the produce.

04 / PARCHMENT

The canvas does most of the work.

ECE9E0 is warm enough to signal provenance and paper memory. Let sections breathe on it. Resist filling the canvas — restraint is the signal.
