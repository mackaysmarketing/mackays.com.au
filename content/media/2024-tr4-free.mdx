title: Mackays verified Panama TR4-free for a third consecutive year
date: 2024-06-18
excerpt: >-
  Independent auditing confirms a third consecutive year free of Panama TR4
  across every Mackays block — a milestone for the Tully Valley and the
  Australian banana industry.
---

Panama TR4 remains the most significant biological threat to the Australian
banana industry, and for the third consecutive year every Mackays block has
been independently verified free of it.

The result reflects a non-negotiable commitment to on-farm biosecurity —
quarantine zones, wash-down stations, visitor restrictions, strict vehicle
protocols and ongoing investment in monitoring.

Mackays thanks its team, its neighbours in the Tully Valley and Biosecurity
Queensland for their shared vigilance.
