The Mackay family business was founded in 1945 by Stanley and Agnes Mackay.
Their sons John and Robert took it into its second generation, and since the
early 2000s the business has been led day-to-day by five third-generation
Mackay brothers — Gavin, Barrie, Stephen, Cameron and Daniel.

Three members of the fourth generation have now formally moved into full-time
roles inside the business, spanning field operations, logistics and agtech.

It is, in the family's words, "exactly the continuity we've been farming
for".
